Fang CFD Analysis

Return to portfolio

Timeframe: Aug 2024 - Present

Skills: Computational Fluid Dynamics, 3D Visualization, Data Analysis

Applications: Biomimetic Injection Design

This was a two-person project that involved taking extensive measurements of various venomous snake fangs for the exploration of snake anatomy.

We first clustered these raw measurements and attempted to find patterns in their biology that could be traced back to common ancestors or shared envenomation techniques. Moving on to build fluid simulations with Ansys Fluent, we were able to better analyze the flow of venom through the fangs and identify key design aspects.

This study can be applied both to the design of biomimetic injections and to building better functional understanding of snake biology.
